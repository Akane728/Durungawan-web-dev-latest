MENU

Durungawan is a progression of rare and beautiful ingredients where texture, flavour and harmony is paramount.

Continually inspired by nature, the creative process for chef Margot begins in working closely with the farmers, fishermen, producers and artisans who cultivate bespoke produce exclusively for Durungawan. For each dish, the growth of every element and the selection of every ceramic piece is carefully considered and crafted for its role in the dining experience.

Durungawan brings a personal, interactive passage for diners through our culinary evolution.

Appetizers

Kamote Quick View

Kamote
Appetizers

In this dish, we present the versatility of the sweet potato through the delivery of the root vegetable in different forms and textures.

Components:

smoked sweet potato. dehydrated sweet potato crisps. sweet potato puree. parsnip crisps. confit sweet potato. sweet potato leaf chips. sweet potato crumble. ancho-honey & dalandan sauce. dehydrated fennel. segmented dalandan.

Appetizers

Jackfruits in coconut Quick View

Jackfruits in coconut
Appetizers

We really love the flavor profile of a famous dish from Romblon, the ginataang langka. Our version showcases one of our most prominent local produce while also showing how we utilize it with both the knowledge of traditional techniques and a modern mind.

Components:

unripe jackfruit and water chestnut stewed in coconut milk. dehydrated coconut shards. fermented krill. caviar.

Entrees

Kinilaw Quick View

Kinilaw
Entrees

Sometimes, cooking doesn't really give the most justice to maximize the experience of eating fish. Kilawin is a traditional Filipino dish that uses raw fish marinated in spiced vinegar, and our version consists of ingredients of only the best quality.

Components:

narrow-barred Spanish mackerel. spiced vinegar. calamansi. ginger. chilli. red onion.

Entrees

Sisig Quick View

Sisig
Entrees

Taking inspiration from one of our most popular local food, our Sisig retains its original identity while amplifying the most important flavor profiles. Also, our Sisig borrows a component from another popular dish, the lechon.

Components:

smoked pig jowl. white onion. crispy pork skin. confit pork belly. clarified pork butter. calamansi sauce.

Desserts

Adobo Quick View

Adobo
Desserts

Our most popular and showstopping dessert, we believe that our Adobo is the epitome of our essence as food artists. Completely novel yet also traditional. A journey over borders & generations.

Components:

soy-braised pork belly. poached pineapple with soy. rosemary ice cream. mixed nut and white chocolate crumble. dehydrated pineapple honeycomb.

Desserts

Tablea Quick View

Tablea
Desserts

To celebrate the presence of tablea in our culture, we decided to simulate the experience of opening a real cocoa pod. From champorado to the suman of Leyte and to many other local dishes, we would like to acknowledge the role tablea plays in making our rich food culture even more special.

Components:

peanut sable. tablea cremeux. dulce de leche. 70% couverture shell. spiced mango

Desserts

Brazo Quick View

Brazo
Desserts

The classic Brazo de mercedes, deconstructed and reintroduced. Unlike the traditional cake, our version combines the sweet subtlety of custard apple with the bright funkiness of guava.

Components:

guava granita. custard apple ice cream. poached meringue. maltose tuiles. vanilla cream. guava puree.

Desserts

Sour candy & rice Quick View

Sour candy & rice
Desserts

A seemingly innocent mandarin sphere that packs a really strong punch! You'll immediately be reminded of a childhood favorite known for its extreme sourness. Although we have made the experience a lot more bearable, this dessert will still definitely be unforgettable.

Components:

mandarin cream. citrus fluid gel. nitro yoghurt foam. rice bubble disc. white cocoa butter coat. sour candy powder. candied orange zest

Desserts

Calamansi meringue tart Quick View

Calamansi meringue

tart
Desserts

Powerful but soothing, our calamansi meringue tart is the perfect mix of refined and comfort eating; with the peanut pastry as a wonderful base, the calamansi curd and its zing, the chocolate and its sweetness, and the ginger jam to balance it all off.

Components:

calamansi curd. torched meringue. peanut shortcrust pastry. tempered chocolate. chocolate gaanche. ginger jam.
